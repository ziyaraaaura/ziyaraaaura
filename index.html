About Me

Yoga Guide, Psychologist, Wellness Facilitator & English Teacher

I am a multidisciplinary professional devoted to nurturing the mind, the body, and the soul through an integrated blend of yoga, counselling psychology, holistic wellness, and education. With a compassionate and mindful approach, I create safe, sacred, and transformative spaces that inspire healing, self-discovery, and inner balance.

Through personalised online sessions, immersive retreats, therapeutic guidance, and mindful learning, I support individuals in reconnecting with their authentic selves and cultivating lasting well-being.

My work bridges ancient wisdom and modern understanding — where yoga nurtures vitality and inner harmony, counselling psychology supports emotional healing and resilience, and English coaching empowers confident communication and expression.

I offer flexible online sessions thoughtfully designed to accommodate diverse lifestyles, schedules, and time zones.

My mission is to guide individuals toward a life of clarity, balance, conscious growth, and soulful transformation.

Prepare Yourself for Online Sessions

  • Keep your yoga mat ready
  • Wear comfortable clothes
  • Be punctual — arrive 10 minutes before class
  • Keep a water bottle nearby
  • Remove ornaments if they feel uncomfortable
  • Choose a quiet, clutter-free room
  • Keep a mobile holder / stand ready
  • Check your network connection before class
  • Put your mobile on silent / Do Not Disturb mode
  • Keep your camera positioned to show full body posture (if posture correction is needed)
  • Avoid heavy meals 1–2 hours before class
  • Keep a small towel / cushion / chair nearby if needed
